Jana Beals...

Currently residing in the small college town of Cheney, Washington Jana divides her time between assisting several non-profit agencies, the Public Relations Student Society of America ("PRSSA"), and her immediate family.

Hobbies include sewing, reading, writing, photography, and playing with her pets.

Jana is a 2015 graduate of Eastern Washington University, "EWU", where she earned a B.A. in Technical Communications, and minored in Visual Communication Design.

She is ultimately seeking to conquer the world of publishing by writing or editing but until that time comes, she will pursue other topics that are equally interesting to her such as public relations, communications, and logo design.
